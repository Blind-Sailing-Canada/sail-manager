 Requesting a new sail

Before requesting a new sail, please check if a similar request was submitted by someone else. You can check latest sail requests by going to dashboard > my sails > sail request. If you find a sail request that matches your needs, you can join it by clicking I'm interested button.

To request a new sail go dashboard > my sails > request a sail. Fill out the form by providing details about the sail. Provide information such as date and time of the sail.

Joining a sail

From main dashboard you can view the next 10 availabe sail. From the available sails list click on a sail which will take you to sail's details page. Review sail's details carefully to make sure it matches your needs. To join this sail click on one of the join as buttons located at the bottom of the page. The type of join as button depends on your membership type.

If you have a question about the sail then you can leave a comment in the sail's details page. Your comment will be sent via email to all participants of this sail.
